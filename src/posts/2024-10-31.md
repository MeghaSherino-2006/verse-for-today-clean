### Verse for Today

> ‘Finally, be strong in the Lord and the strength of His might’ (Ephesians 6:10)

---

### Reflection

You and I are asked to embody the Lord and His strength this day. To stand firm, to stand our ground, to be fearless when perplexities hit hard, we need power and inspiration. Every day is an opportunity to live the Word, to fearlessly make known the mystery of our Salvation and this as St Paul did we are called to do. To be ambassadors of Christ, but within the limits and limitations of our vocation. To be a votary for the Lord is not a simple task and those who respond to this call from the Lord, are strengthened by Him. A Christ ambassador is aware of his strengths and weaknesses. He is aware of the limitation of time and consults with the Holy Spirit of how he could best make use of his time. Wherever he finds himself, and with whomever he dwells, his refuge and strength is in his trust in the Lord. That even if the world or situations turn hostile, the One who is with him is always in full control and at his call when in need. It is into this kind of a trust You and I have been called to renew and grow ourselves into. Be strong in the Lord and in the strength of His might. Let this be our prayer for the day to renew ourselves as soldiers and ambassadors of Jesus. Amen
