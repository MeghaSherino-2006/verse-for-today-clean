### Verse for Today

> ‘Submit yourselves, then, to God. Resist the devil, and he will flee from you’ (James 4:7)

---

### Reflection

The Holy Spirit advises us to ‘submit’ to the Lord, no matter how difficult, how weak, incapable, or distraught – because we belong to God. It is the Lord who awaits to see us bloom, to spread the effervescence of love, hope, and faith; and thus to see us bear fruit in every season of life. Whenever in the dark, when in doubt, when disturbed, remember that the enemy is at work, under cover, and he intends only to steal, to rob, and destroy all that the Lord has offered to us. But when in ‘dire’ state, we are often tempted to abandon the Lord, to break our bonds with Him in prayer, to put the blame on Him for all the untoward. Let us humbly submit ourselves before Jesus, the ‘One’ who ransomed us by a sacrifice of self to the Father. Let us draw near to Him in our ‘own ways’ - You and I are unique, and so is the way we converse in prayer. Let praise, thanksgiving, and supplications be poured from our hearts to the Lord, who delights to hear from us. This is how we learn to resist the evil one, who will be forced to flee from us. Wishing you a blessed morning! Amen
