### Verse for Today

> ‘Therefore, if you have any encouragement from being united with Christ, if any comfort from His love, if any common sharing in the Spirit, if any tenderness and compassion…’ (Philippians 2:1)

---

### Reflection

The Holy Spirit enlightens us on what we transform to when united with the Lord – He fills you with encouragement to do everything you have been called to do; His love will surprise you and comfort you while you go though rough patches in life; He opens up His Spirit to you generously; Your heart blends with His in tenderness, compassion and mercy – abundance of life. Thus, Jesus asks you, what does one profit if He merely gains the whole world but loses His life? How could one ever pay the price of His life, a gift from the Heavenly Father. For all good things come from the Father of Lights, our Heavenly Father, St James reminds us. Jesus today calls you to a transition, to imitate Him, so that you attain and exhibit the distinction that defines who you really are. Not to be a carbon copy of another human, but to reflect Divinity in all that you say and do. You have a royal heritage, are a priestly nation, and God’s treasured possession – therefore put on Christ this very day and live your call to its fullest. May Jesus be your guide today. Amen
