### Verse for Today

> ‘As a deer pants for flowing streams, so pants my soul for you, O God. My soul thirsts for God, for the Living God’ (Psalm 42:1,2)

---

### Reflection

I thought to myself why does this deer pant for flowing streams as it moves through the forest? And this was what the Spirit of the Lord caused me to understand – Just as You and I may have to put up with trials in life, temptations we would need to flee from, difficulties we would need to endure, the deer is chased by predators mightier than him. The animal would always need to be on high alert, defend itself from dangerous situations and therefore would pant for streams of flowing waters, where it expects to see animals of its kind. You and I are this deer that thirsts for the Living Waters that flow from the heart of the Lord – the Holy Spirit. It is here that we are filled, replenished, recharged and reset to take our next move in life’s onerous crossroads. The Lord is our stronghold, our protection and our powerhouse. Let us learn to lean on Him this day, asking from Him the power to quench all worldly deprivations in us and to gear up for today’s new tasks he desires us to take hands-on, to succeed with Him for His glory. Amen
