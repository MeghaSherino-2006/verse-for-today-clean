### Verse for Today

> ‘For this reason Christ is the mediator of a new Covenant, that those who are called may receive the promised Eternal inheritance – now He has died as a ransom to set them free from the sins committed under the First Covenant’ (Hebrews 9:15)

---

### Reflection

You and I have been called to be partakers of the New Covenant, because of Jesus Christ. He is the reason why we stand worthy (freed from our sins and transgressions), not in any Holy place, but in the Heavens before the presence of God our Father. We have been reconciled to God the Father; Our prayers and supplications have been made eligible to be presented to Him, through the Holy Spirit who intercedes on our behalf and because of the strength of the mediation of Jesus, who is now with the Father. His precious blood which was shed for the redemption of all mankind, in the due course of time - the appointed time of the Father, which was introduced within the limits of time and space on earth, thus made tangible to history, once and for all, the blood of the New Covenant. That His beloved ones, (You and me) who found ourselves distanced from our Salvation, because of our incapacity to obey the Old Covenantal laws, would be reclaimed through His sacrifice. Let us therefore plead before the Throne of the Most High for mercy, forgiveness, and grace to live without blemishes, away from all works of the flesh (dead works) to serve the Living God in all that we are. Amen
