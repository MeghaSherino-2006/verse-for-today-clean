### Verse for Today

> 'For who sees anything different in you? What did you have that you did not receive? And if you received it why do you boast as if it were not a gift? (1 Corinthians 4:7)

---

### Reflection

All that is ours is a gift - our wealth, talents, relationships, opportunities, our way of living. Even the generosity we express, those things we acclaim as obtained by 'hook or crook' were given to us - if not all the hard work and prayers offered would have left us still seeking for them. About tomorrow we have no idea about, about the very next moment, we hardly can imagine, but we tend to count ourselves distinct and worthy of them all. More than all, we go the extra mile (not to support another less privileged, but) to judge, allege him/ her as solely responsible for his depravity, of course with little knowledge of the whole picture. It is then that Jesus brings to us the parable of the Pharisee and the tax collector - telling us that this tax collector who humbled himself before God was the one who was more justified before the Almighty, than the one who (was self sufficient) and made sure he never missed a single rite or ritual to be appreciated by the crowds. Jesus asks us to look into ourselves in this light and reach out to Him for grace. Wishing you a blessed Friday. Amen
