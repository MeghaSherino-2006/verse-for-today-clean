### Verse for Today

> ' Even in the darkness light dawns for the upright, for those who are gracious, compassionate and righteous' (Psalm 112:4)

---

### Reflection

The Upright are those who fear the Lord, those who delight to do His will. For these, whom the Psalmist addresses as 'the Blessed', - in the dark will light dawn. Their hearts are firm in the Hands of the Lord, that helps them recover from all that threatens. God's Spirit calls us too to be the Upright, those gracious, compassionate and passionate for righteousness; and then look to the Lord. For nothing guile or impure can imbibe/exhibit the glory of God. What do you and I regard as more precious today? The love of the Lord or of men. Everything changes and is subject to the law of change, except for the Word of God and His great promises. Let us learn to seek the Lord at all times with a pure heart, seek to be filled by His perfection, nothing else. Amen
