### Verse for Today

> 'For your steadfast love is as high as the Heavens; your faithfulness extends to the clouds' (Psalm 57:10)

---

### Reflection

When all that constitutes our skies fail to yield the expected fruits, we humans tend to acknowledge that our skies are inadequate. David, the psalmist once prayed ' that the power of the Most High is not manifest in me is the cause of my grief'. And thus he asks God to envelope his skies with glory. His conviction was that God would soon send from the Heavens, His saving power. To David this expressed itself in the steadfast love and faithfulness of God. To You and me, the Lord Jesus is the same yesterday, today and tomorrow. His saving power, steadfast love and faithfulness would reach us the moment we call upon His name.His name is strength in weakness, abundance of peace when our hearts are disturbed and inevitable power in times of need. May your steadfast love O Lord reach up to the Heavens and faithfulness touch the clouds. Thankyou Lord
Amen
