### Verse for Today

> ‘They will see the glory of the Lord, the majesty of our God’ (Isaiah 35:2)

---

### Reflection

This was Heaven’s standard – the lowly things of this world were chosen, the despised, the things that were not, to bring to nothing the things that were…. But to us humans, who focus on things that matter, God sent His only Son in human form, that we may learn what it is to be human-like. Thus, the prophets spoke of the times when the Messiah comes, that the wilderness and dry land will be made glad, deserts would rejoice and be made fruitful, and the whole creation would be mesmerized at God’s great works of wonder. It is in this context that we are told to rejoice, no matter how things may seem indifferent to us, to garner up strength, as when the Lord comes, He will come with His recompense. The Lord comes to establish His reign over mankind, His holiness and righteousness. He comes to ransom all that was confiscated, deprived, and lost. Beyond the confines of time and space, this restoration of Jesus still occurs in our midst, in our hearts, minds, and homes, whenever we joyfully exalt His greatness. So, along with St Paul, let us also preach the message of the Cross to the world that let the one who boasts, boast in the Lord. WE humbly await your glorious coming into hearts, O Lord. Amen
