### Verse for Today

> ‘Awesome is God from His Sanctuary – the God of Israel – He is the One who gives power and strength to His people. Blessed be God!’(Psalm 68:35)

---

### Reflection

Today’s Word from the Lord is that of strength and empowerment. Whenever we learn to be exultant in God, when we sing praises to His Holy name, we rejoice in Him, He comes down to our midst from His Sanctuary. The Sanctuary of God is a refuge for the Fatherless, the widow and the destitute – those who are oppressed and have none to heed to their cries. With abundance and joy, God fills the hearts of those who seek after Him. God rebukes the beasts, the Evil that exist in our midst that chokes and deprives us from enjoying sheer abundance. He wills to dwell in our midst, however unworthy we are, He restores our inheritance from its state of deterioration. He sets our feet on level ground, to a new start each new day. The tempo in us He sustains and invigorates; training us each day to hope even when things get behind the very essence of hope. For it is His will that we wait in patient forbearance, striving in prayer and effort to see His great works start to blossom in our lives. If You and I have offered ourselves into the Hands of our Lord, believe and convince yourself that He ‘Himself’ will instruct you as to the Way that must be yours today. Amen
