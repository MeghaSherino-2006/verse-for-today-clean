### Verse for Today

> ' He chose our heritage for us, the pride of Jacob whom He loves' (Psalm 47:4)

---

### Reflection

Jacob the younger of Isaac's sons, was known as the schemer, the one who usurped the patriarchal blessings.....but his ways  never gave him rest, as for 20 long years he had to pay the price of his deeds, reparation for sins. It was at God's destined time, in accordance to His plan, when Jacob submitted, he began to enjoy his heritage, the privilege God chose to reward him and his descendants. Thus Jacob returned to his ancestors in double glory, the glory that the Lord wrapped him with, which was the portion for Israel. All that you and I have been going through was a preparation to submit ourselves, received from above, admit our weaknesses, and obtain our privileged inheritance from God. The  moment we reconcile our thought processes, perspectives and dreams into the hands of the Almighty, we ready ourselves to receive our portion from God. Irrespective of what our present circumstances may seem, or what many have told us, You and I stay a chance to inherit greater things, that we never imagined, or dreamed about, because we have Jesus who loves us as none can . Amen
