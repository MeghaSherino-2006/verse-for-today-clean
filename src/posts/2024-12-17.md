### Verse for Today

> 'Make me to know thy ways, O LORD; teach me thy paths. Lead me in thy truth, and teach me, for thou art the God of my salvation' (Psalm 25:4-5)

---

### Reflection

Thankyou Lord for this new day you have given me. I pray that I learn more of what please you, to do thy will. Lead me through the path I must tread this day, encourage me to always lean on to the truth, that comes from you and not on my understanding. Enhance the boundaries of my intellect, my understanding, my abilities to love and serve you more. May your steadfast love keep drawing me close to you, though I may fail at times. Be at my side to instruct, guide and help me fulfill my purpose of life this day. In Jesus name I ask. Amen
