### Verse for Today

> ‘For the desires of the flesh are against the Spirit, and the desires of the Spirit are against the flesh, for these are opposed to each other, to keep you from doing the things you want to do’ (Galatians 5:17)

---

### Reflection

Jesus knew the vulnerability of the human mind, which always leaned towards being independent, but not in its complete sense. True independence and freedom of the mind would only spring from its sense of doing its purpose. Whatever else one does will never give him/her a sense of content. Therefore, to his disciples, Jesus exhorted them to pray and unceasingly pray to ensure they do not miss their purpose by doing anything and everything. You and I are called to do those specific good deeds that were predestined for us when we were created by His Word, Jesus Christ. It is the Holy Spirit that helps us discern what the good deeds assigned to our vocation are. These may not seem lucrative in the eyes of the world. The desires of the flesh are always opposed to the desires of the Spirit of God in us. Let us ask the Lord to enlighten and strengthen us to do the things we are called to do, to keep from doing what is not required of us, and be His messengers on earth. Amen
