### Verse for Today

> 'For judgment will be without mercy to anyone who has shown no mercy; mercy triumphs over judgment' (James 2:13)

---

### Reflection

Going by the standards of this world, it may seem ridiculously strange to be merciful, especially when by all means of the law (we are subject to), the provisions or possibilities of remission  has exhausted. Even in the realm of religion, adhering to rules and bylaws get predominance over the needed concern for the other. Thus we have failed to honour Christ and for the principles He stood for. Jesus reminds us that every moment in time in creation, there is this groaning for redemption. The opportunities where we have failed our egos were those where we resembled the work of Christ - His redemption. Such is the significance of mercy that purports to it a genre and authority over any other. Prompted by love does spring mercy and to all who have triumphed to love have gained the perfection that cleanses all imperfections and sin. Therefore let us pray for a heart that is softened by compassion and love, that promotes unity, harmony, and the good in another. This is when we attain the likeness of Christ our Master. Amen
