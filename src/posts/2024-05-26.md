### Verse for Today

> ‘Trust in the Lord, and do good; dwell in the land and befriend faithfulness’ (Psalm 37:3)

---

### Reflection

Faithfulness and abundance come from the Lord. Jesus is the fountain and from Him, we partake in the Living waters, the Holy Spirit that enlightens us to see the Light, the Truth and the way ahead in life’s journey. Contrary to logic or common reason (that we have obtained from the ways of the world) surety, calm, abundance and delights are what percolates deep into a soul that trusts in the Lord. Where the Spirit of the Lord dwells, there is freedom and hope. This freedom creates in us an aversion to sinfulness; while hope reminds us of God’s great promises to those who delight in Him. Therefore, this Sunday morning, Jesus teaches us what it means to ‘delight in Him’ – to rest in Him, to wait patiently for Him. By this, we befriend ourselves to the Father, because He has given us Jesus, that we may inherit Eternity. We become one with the Holy Trinity, the great mystery Jesus revealed to us through His Baptism. On this day of the Feast of the Holy Trinity, may God be our share, our fortress, refuge and hope for a better tomorrow. Over all that is transient, Jesus has given us mastery to conquer, because of His steadfast love and faithfulness for us. Amen
