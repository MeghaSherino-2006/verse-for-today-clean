### Verse for Today

> ‘Let us who are of the day, be sober, putting on the breastplate of faith and love; and for a helmet, the hope of Salvation’ (1 Thessalonians 5:8)

---

### Reflection

We who have experienced the Light of Jesus are called to preserve the light and its intensity in us to the end. God has destined us for Salvation through Jesus Christ and this is not a one-shot-stop effort. It calls for continuity, renewal of strength and power in the Holy Spirit each day. And that which keeps us on track is these 3 Theological virtues – faith, Hope and Love – It is these that pose the yardstick for our assessment, as to whether we have fallen from the Light. If faith in the Lord Jesus, which inevitably would mean ‘trust in Him’ has dwindled, if love for the Lord has become cold in us, if hope of the Glory of ‘Being Saved’ has lost its severity in us, it is sure test that we have deteriorated in our soberness of being the Children of Light. In case, we are, where do we turn to? God’s Word and His promises. These hold immense power to rejuvenate and reignite us in the Holy Spirit. Ask yourself, how much have we longed to follow the Lord, after all that He has done for us. Jesus asks us of nothing more than to trust in Him. Amen
