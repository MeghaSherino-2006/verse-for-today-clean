### Verse for Today

> 'All His precepts are trustworthy. They are established forever and ever, to be performed with faithfulness and uprightness' (Psalm 111:7,8)

---

### Reflection

Irrespective of time, space and technological progress, that which remains constant is the Divine law. His precepts that teach mankind how to live as per their call in life were established for Eternity. It is from these precepts that wisdom is imparted into the human heart. These were given to His own - Jacob/ Israel, and through them to the ends of the world through Jesus, His Son. That we may understand them, honour and obey these in faithfulness and in its fullness. These protect one from being evaded by evil and strengthens His Inner being to fight the battle with courage and conviction. For our meditation today, our Good Lord asks how determined we are in keeping His Word. For the Heavens and earth may pass by but not a single word that He has dictated.Let us seek help from the Holy Spirit to revere Jesus and abide by His Word always. Amen
