### Verse for Today

> ‘He who descended is the one who also ascended far above all the Heavens, that He might fill all things’ (Ephesians 4:10)

---

### Reflection

He who descended to be with us was Jesus Christ, the only Son of the Father. He descended that we might know the truth, the love and mercy of God for mankind. We see a God who came down to redeem man, to save him from his sins, transgressions and the destructions he was subject to. There may be situations in life irreversible, some which others blame us as responsible for, those we have no clue for a comeback. In all these, He the Lord is ready to redeem and restore. We must always remember that as He descended, He also ascended gloriously to the Heavens, that every spiritual and material blessing lost to us may be restored. He gave gifts to men, as He ascended, the resurrected Christ. Therefore, whatever may be our vocation in life, whatever may be our trials and difficulties in life, He knows it all. Let us thus ask the One who holds the key to the Heavens and Earth to open for us greater gifts that we ask for today. With faith and conviction we pray, Lord, answer our prayers. Amen
