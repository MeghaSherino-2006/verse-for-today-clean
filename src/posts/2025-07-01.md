### Verse for Today

> ‘The Angels urged Lot……..but he hesitated, so the men seized him and his wife and his two daughters by the hand, the Lord being merciful to him, and they brought him out and set him outside the city’ (Genesis 19:15-16)

---

### Reflection

The Lord was merciful to them and thus the two angels led Lot, his wife, and his two daughters by the hand safely out of the city of punishment. They were hesitant and lingered for, to their eyes the massive wealth, property, and security of a home (at Sodom, the city that God was about to punish) was what meant everything to them. We know the context of Abraham interceding for his nephew Lot, and for the land before God, when God made known to him, His plan to destroy the city on account of the sinfulness of the people. For us to meditate upon this morning is that we too like Lot have credited the mercy of God, not because we were worthy of it, but because of Jesus and for the ransom He paid for us, to set us free from the wrath of God that was to fall upon all mankind. Therefore, having understood of God’s magnanimity, and graciousness in Jesus Christ, we are obliged like Lot (who was asked to flee from the city to another land) to live lives worthy of God’s will and to seek what pleases Him the most. Let us ask the Lord Jesus, to grace us with the gifts of His Spirit – wisdom, counsel, knowledge, understanding, fear of God, fortitude, and piety, that we may do the little we can today for God’s kingdom, to share the joy, peace, and forgiveness of the Lord to those given to us, and to be humble to reap the fruits of His favour for us. Amen
