### Verse for Today

> ‘If you ask me anything in my name, I will do it’ (John 14:14)

---

### Reflection

To ask in Jesus’s name would mean more than adding it to the end of our prayers. It would infer a humble submission to the will of the Father for us; accepting that we are inadequate due to our sinfulness; believing that Jesus has claimed for us a treasure highly esteemed, the position as sons and daughters of God. In no other name is our Salvation – in no person, relationship, authority or power, but in Jesus. Therefore, being born again in Jesus and His Spirit, we are called towards an extraordinary lifestyle – one that follows after Jesus, imitates His ways and transforms into His likeliness each day. It is in our calling to hold firmly to what we have heard in the beginning, what has drawn us to faith in Jesus, to love Him with a pure heart, and to let go of ways, practices and ideals that are contrary to proclaiming Jesus as the Lord. It is thus that when we ask in Jesus’s name, we are healed, we are delivered from the bondages of this world, and we enjoy the fruits of His Spirit in us. To all who thirst to seek the truth, He gives this privilege – To dwell in His Word and keep asking for in His name. Amen
