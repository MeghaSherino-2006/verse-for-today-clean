### Verse for Today

> '....Jesus showed himself again to the disciples by the Sea of Tiberias, He showed Himself in this way..... Just after day break Jesus stood on the beach, but the disciples did not know that it was Jesus' (John 21:1-4)

---

### Reflection

This third appearance that Jesus made before his disciples was different. And we understand this because, though the disciples saw Jesus standing at the beach shore, they could not recognise Him. We too at times of distress, when buried deep within our struggles and challenges fail to see the  never failing presence of the Lord with us. It could be to experientially teach His disciples that Jesus led through a situation of despair, of faithlessness.  To comprehend the fact that they would need to seek the Lord at all times , not look into themselves and who they were...that Jesus permitted them to fall back to their old ways
 Whenever distraught and insecure in life, You and I are asked to patiently wait for the Lord trusting in His unwavering concern for us at all times. He would be just there and at the right moment to turn out the most needed miracle for us at that hour. Let's pray this Sunday for the grace to long after Him always, to learn to lean on Him every moment this day. Lord, we offer this day to you. Strengthen and encourage us to be faithful to you. Amen
