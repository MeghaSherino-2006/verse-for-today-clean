### Verse for Today

> ‘For You granted Him authority over all people that He might give eternal life to all those You have given Him’ (John 17:2)

---

### Reflection

Jesus was praying for Himself, for all those whom God had granted to Him for redemption. And we know that His prayers were heard – He was glorified by the Father after His suffering, passion, and death, through a resurrection unheard of by humanity till date. This is our assurance that we are eligible to Eternal life, provided we accept Jesus, as Lord and Master of our lives – To Him has been given authority over all people. With this in mind, let us approach His Throne of Mercy with the confidence that He will never let down anyone who approaches Him. He who has authority over all and to judge all, will hear our prayers, honour them in His due time. Let us thus pray for ourselves, to be washed, sanctified, renewed and reclaimed for His glory (through these days of penance and reparation), let us pray for those given to our lives – our dear and near – within our families, extended families, those far from us but close to our hearts, those whom the Lord brings to us this day, those memories and moments of the past that have often been offending to us, those whom we owe forgiveness and those to whom we need to forgive wholeheartedly, those around us who have supported us and support us to this day, those in administration, our leaders – socio-political, and others whom we remember, that they may also receive the abundance of life, Eternity, by being truthful to their call, and worthy by way of their deeds, that all may know and acclaim Jesus, as Lord and master for a new and better ecosystem for all humanity – Thy Kingdom come, O Lord, on earth as it is in the Heavens. Amen
