### Verse for Today

> 'I will turn all my mountains into a road and my highways shall be raised up' (Isaiah 49:11)

---

### Reflection

The mountains are the blocks that prevent you and me from connecting with God. Today the Lord  says, He, (who made the stars, the skies, the earth and all) will make these obstacles as paths to reach Him. It is Jesus, who initiates this transition, He will cause the pride in us to break and give way to seek Him. All those that were rewarding, that we believed to be fruitful, the inclinations of our intellect and mind, He will cause to be destroyed that we embrace Him the true light, the cause of our redemption. The more You and I express ourselves as worthy and eligible before God, we lose our chances of being used by Him. Let this day be an opportune moment to sit with the Lord, walk closely with Him, and obey every Word that comes from Him, letting go of all ego, all the strongholds that dissuade us to stay the course with Him. Jesus will turn all mountains into a road, will make the way for us to meet Him face to face just in time, to be with Him at all times. Lord, you are sufficient for me this time and ever. Amen
