### Verse for Today

> ‘All my springs are in you’ (Psalm 87:7)

---

### Reflection

Springs are symbolic of inner joy, peace, beauty, and sound health. In the course of time, seasons do change; summers, autumns, and winters give way again to the advent of spring. The delight, hope, beauty, and energy of Spring revitalize our spirits, but what makes it explicitly known to us is its distinctness from other seasons. Likewise, the adversities in life, pain, and suffering are a reality, but the hope of an upcoming reward or greater glory can nullify all these troubles, equipping one with the needed strength to move on. This hope is what we find in Christ Jesus, and when the Lord comes He brings to us inexpressible springs of joy and peace. The Spirit of God teaches us where to reach out for happiness, for contentment, for encouragement, and courage whenever life goes down the lane, may seem merely black and white, and even disoriented and all alone. The soul that trusts in the Lord will always revive in strength, will capture the best in memories, and radiate it as light to those around. May you be like this one. Amen
