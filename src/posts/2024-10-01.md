### Verse for Today

> ‘You may have strength to comprehend with all the saints what is the breadth and length and height and depth and to know the love of Christ that surpasses knowledge, that you may be filled with all the fullness of God’ (Ephesians 3:18)

---

### Reflection

Today God’s Word points to where exactly our issue in life lies in – Despite being recipients of so great a love, one which wiped all our sins, took our burdens, blessed us with all material and spiritual things we may never have asked for. We fail to realize the breadth, height, length, and depth of this love of the Lord for each one of us. Instead, we keep justifying ourselves, attributing the cause and reason (for our failures) not to our reckless living, but to the loving merciful Lord who has been gracious to us. And this explains the key difference between the Saints and us, who claim to be followers of Christ. So let us pray this morning for the strength – for the conviction that dominated the minds and hearts of saints, to know Jesus, the love He has for us, ‘the knowledge’ that takes us overboard to live our purpose with courage and determination, which would gradually lead us phase by phase towards the fullness of God, which is Christ in us. Let this knowledge of the love of Christ ignite us to initiate acts of charity, that which may be of no meaning to those around, but which will always be remembered and honored by the Lord, Most High. Amen
