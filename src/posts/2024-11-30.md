### Verse for Today

> 'Learn where there is wisdom, where there is strength, where there is understanding, so that you may at the same time discern where there is length of days and life, where there is light for the eyes and peace' (Baruch 3:14)

---

### Reflection

Generations from old, those who lived upon the earth, were unaware of wisdom, of understanding and of the source of them all. It was because the One who possessed the storehouse of wisdom, desired to give it to those who were chosen. It was to Jacob that God showered his endless, priceless blessing- to him and his descendants. All wisdom is comprised in the Commandment He gave Israel. To love Him and to love our fellow beings has been the command He has given to you and me, the New Israel. From this comes the gift of discernment, the gift of Light and peace. This is why the Lord exhorted His beloved - Turn O Jacob, take Her, walk towards the shining of Her Light, Do not give your glory to another ...
Let us thank Jesus for this gift of wisdom that is in Him and with Him. Let us pray to be filled with Jesus, this being the glory we have received from God and our advantage over all. Amen
