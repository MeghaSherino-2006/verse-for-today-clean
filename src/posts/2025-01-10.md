### Verse for Today

> 'For he strengthens the bars of your gates; he blesses your sons within you. He makes peace in your borders; he fills you with the finest of the wheat' (Psalm 147:13,14)

---

### Reflection

For the warmth of His protection, for all the blessings He opens to the people given to us, for the prosperity and material sufficiency He provides us, many are the reasons for which we are obliged to glorify the Lord. Along with all creation, for the redemption and forgiveness He has caused us to inherit, for Jesus, His Son by whom we have been made Sons and daughters of the Heavenly kingdom, we must give praise and thank His Holy name. That through the mystery of Christ revealed to us, we (who were Gentiles) have become heirs to the promises given to the Patriarchs, we believe and  accept these from God, the Father. No more to live as aliens or strangers in the Kingdom, but as partners with the saints, to be merciful and receive mercy. This is our resolve, we make with thanksgiving to the Lord for all that He has graced us( adorned our human nature) with. Glory be to the Father, to the Son and to the Holy Spirit, for ever. Amen
