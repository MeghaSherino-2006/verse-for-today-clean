### Verse for Today

> 'May I never boast of anything except the Cross of our Lord Jesus Christ, by which the world has been crucified to me, and I to the world' (Galatians 6:14)

---

### Reflection

When in Christ Jesus, all other priorities of earthly living orient themselves, meaning they get aligned  orderly to serve the purpose of life - the glorification of God. St Paul, an authority in the Laws of Moses, was once set to prove himself (docile and fervent to fulfill the Laws), but when Christ came to his life, he was never concerned of proving himself but in working relentlessly for the Lord and His kingdom. He was focused and true to his purpose, never again had to waste time and effort towards his objective, as the Lord worked with signs to confirm his authority. This gave Paul the conviction that every other thing was irrelevant before the message of His Saviour. This trust in the loving providence of Jesus, is what he exhorts us to upgrade to - a living faith. In the Cross of Jesus Christ is our cause for boasting, as he has accomplished for us all that we ask in prayer and by which we are made eligible to receive. Praise the Lord. Amen
