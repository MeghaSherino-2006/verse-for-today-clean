### Verse for Today

> ‘For surely it is not angels that he helps, but he helps the offspring of Abraham’ (Hebrews 2:16)

---

### Reflection

God wanted mankind to realize their worth – for His Son was sent to redeem mankind. Not to save Angels, did Jesus take the form of a man, but to reclaim the lost you and me to the Father. To restore us to our original selves, He became fully human in every way, to become that merciful and faithful High Priest in service to God, that atonement for the sins of all men would be performed in its fullness. He himself was tempted by the evil one, therefore He is able to understand us whenever we reach out to Him for solace and support. Not a mere understanding, but also wields the authority to transform every creation to submit to His plan. None can supersede His plans or purpose for our lives. He, the Good Shepherd knows His sheep, their strengths and weaknesses, that they tend to go astray, that He would need to seek after them. Let us pray to this Good Shepherd Jesus, who tends to us day and night, provides for our needs and protects us from all that is hazardous to life. A battered reed He would not break, a smoldering wick He will not extinguish until He leads justice to victory for you and me. In trust and confidence in Him will be our peace. Amen
