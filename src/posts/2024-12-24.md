### Verse for Today

> 'For the yoke of their burden, and bar across their shoulders, the rod of their oppressor, you have broken as on the day of Midian' (Isaiah 9:4)

---

### Reflection

These three blessings have been conferred to us, because of the child born to us this day in the House of David in Bethlehem - Jesus Christ. As Isaiah prophesied those who were in darkness saw the light of life and great hope. The Wonderful Counselor, Mighty God, Everlasting father, The Prince of Peace will rule over all mankind to deliver, free, heal and elevate them as sons of the Most High. Thus what was spoken to the shepherds is relevant and everlasting to all men of all times. Glory to God in the Highest Heavens and peace to all on earth with goodwill. The Word has become flesh to dwell with us throughout to the end. Let us thank God the Father for His indescribable gift of Jesus. Merry Christmas! Amen
