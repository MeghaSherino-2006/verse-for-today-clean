### Verse for Today

> ‘God being rich in mercy, because of the great love with which he loved us, even when we were dead in our trespasses, made us alive together with Christ – by grace you have been saved’ (Ephesians 2:4,5)

---

### Reflection

We have been raised and enlivened by God our Father, because He loved us so much. It was Jesus who gave up Himself for our elevation. How grateful we must be to the Lord for what He has secured for us, not that we deserved it, but because of the desire of His Father. In Jesus, we have become heirs to everything, beyond what this world can offer us. And the best is yet to come – His immeasurable riches of glory in Christ Jesus, to be unveiled to us as time unfolds. By grace, that is God’s power and will, we have been redeemed for Heaven, to live lives worthy of what we have been gifted. Hardly do we realize our gifts, rather do we care about them! Lord Jesus, open our eyes to see and understand what we have, why we have been given them, why we stand distinct from the rest and what we have been called to attain on this life’s journey. Make each one of us a blessing for those around us. However small or irrelevant we may at times feel, may our roles be played to its fullest for the greater glory of the Lord and for salvation of many. Amen
