### Verse for Today

> For the death He died he died to sin, once for all, but the life He lives he lives to God. So, you also must consider yourselves dead to sin and alive to God in Christ Jesus  
> **[Romans 6:10,11]**

---

### Reflection

Sin had once and for all times lost dominion in the lives of mankind because of the sacrifice of Jesus Christ. Not that men would not sin anymore, but that those who believed in Jesus had been empowered to destroy sin while still living in the world.

Jesus prayed for His disciples in His Priestly prayer that they would not be taken out of the world but kept from the evil one. This remains true for us today — that we are no longer slaves to sin, no longer driven by anger, fear, anxiety, or division.

We are called to embrace our true identity as followers of Christ — to live above these things, in freedom, in goodness, and in love. Every opportunity in life becomes a chance to reflect Christ.

---

###  Prayer

Lord Jesus, enlighten our hearts and minds with your Spirit to embrace our true identity of resembling Christ, the beloved of His Father. Amen.
