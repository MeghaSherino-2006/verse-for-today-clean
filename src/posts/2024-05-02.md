### Verse for Today

> ‘Our Gospel came to you not simply in word, but also with power, with the Holy Spirit, and deep conviction’ (1 Thessalonians 1:5)

---

### Reflection

Every time the Word of God is read, heard, or preached, a four-fold blessing descends upon the one who listens earnestly and the one who spreads the Word. The grace to speak with God’s approval, the truth and not to please men; power from God that unveils in boldness to speak amid adversities; to wield power in the Holy Spirit to love, care and gently share; with assurance that it is God who would intervene in lives of people to equip them for His Kingdom. To Paul, He witnessed to this Word which was fulfilled in the Thessalonians believers. They believed in the Lord Jesus and held firm to faith even when persecutions came their way. They imitated the good works of Paul as he went about toiling and exhorting them like a father to his children. Rest was the work of the Holy Spirit, who fulfilled God’s purpose about the Thessalonians. In the absence of Paul and his co-workers, who had to flee due to persecution there, the community flourished like anything, with hope and love. Let us pray for God’s Word to penetrate deep into our hearts, change our old ways and prepare us for newer things in life. Amen
