### Verse for Today

> ' Jesus asked them this question: What do you think of the Messiah? Whose son is he?' ( Matthew 22:41-42)

---

### Reflection

The same question Jesus asks you and me, What do you think of Him, the Messiah? Whose son is he? Whenever we are lost, lonely, desperate, or even at our best, the Saviour is with us. But have we tried to reach out to him, than confine Him  merely to the Tabernacle (his sure resting place)? Jesus loves and lives in me. He is the King of Kings and the Lord of Lords. Everything created bows before Him, He who came down  assuming the form of a human to transform all that constitutes our world. So the question remains, Are you ready to entrust all that is yours and seek mercy from Him? And when this King is pleased, He bestows every perfect blessing upon you....much more than what you may ask for. Jesus, Son of David, have mercy on me. Amen
