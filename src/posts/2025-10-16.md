### Verse for Today

> ‘God’s righteousness, in His ‘Divine forbearance’ He had passed over former sins. It was to show His righteousness at the ‘present time’, so that He might be just and the justifier of the one who has faith in Jesus’ (Romans 3:25,26)

---

### Reflection

This is the righteousness of God, that all our past transgressions He has ignored, the moment we repented, and after having known Jesus, our Lord, we have obtained the justification that comes from faith. This justification is added grace that inhibits us from falling into sin, avoiding all situations that cause us to sin (sin of omission and commission), being more grateful to Jesus who has accomplished for us this privilege of reconciliation with the Father. Let us be obliged to God, for His goodness, His protection, His longing to see with Him through Jesus. Let us praise and glorify God, making every effort to offer all that we do for His greater glory, seeking to be blessed more by forgiving others who have impeded our good, for this is what the Lord expects from us – To be merciful as the Heavenly Father is. Amen
