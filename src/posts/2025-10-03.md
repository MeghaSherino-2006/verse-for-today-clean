### Verse for Today

> ' I love those who love me........'(Proverbs 8:17)

---

### Reflection

The Wisdom that created the Universe and sustains it is Jesus. He speaks to you and me this morning, giving us the assurance that He cares for us, and provides for us each day. A list of graces He opens to all mankind who honour Him in word and deed -  (I)Riches, honour, enduring wealth, prosperity
(II) Fruit and yield that are more valuable than fine gold and silver 
(III) He prospers them and heaps up their treasures
(IV) Transforms knowledge to profound understanding in those who love Him
(V) To the one bowed by anxieties and worries of life, comfort and courage of conviction is the strength given
From one end of the earth to the other, the Lord seeks for the one who loves Him, to help, honour, and uplift Him....whatever be the anguish in his heart, the losses or unfulfilled dreams of his heart, the Lord will uphold and elevate Him in His due time. Teach us to patiently wait for you O Lord. Amen
