### Verse for Today

> ‘In everything David did, he gave thanks and praise to the Holy Lord, the Most High. He loved his Creator and sang praises to him with all his heart’ (Sirach 47:8)

---

### Reflection

What distinguished David from his predecessor and successors was this – He loved his Creator and sang praises to him wholeheartedly. Not that he was above board in his human nature, he was as feeble and vulnerable as You and me. But what He did for glorifying the Lord, was what endeared him to God. Thus we read in verse 11 that the Lord forgave his sins, and established his power for ever. It was with David that the Lord entered into a Covenant on behalf of his descendants, that his dynasty’s reign would endure through Israelite history. And so it was. Jesus, the Son of God took birth from this dynasty, as the Son of David. During his public ministry, the crowds who were benefitted through the works of Jesus, his miracles for them, addressed him as ‘The Son of David’. Such was the commendation King David received even after his death. As we honour the Lord this Sunday morning, let us pray for his intervention in our lives as the Son of David. So let us pray, Jesus, Son of David, Have mercy on us! Amen
