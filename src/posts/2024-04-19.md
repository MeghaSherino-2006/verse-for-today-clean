### Verse for Today

> ‘And you know the way to where I am going’ (John 14:4)

---

### Reflection

Jesus knew He was on His way to fulfill His purpose in life. He had told his disciples of the suffering, humiliation, death and resurrection he was to undergo soon in Jerusalem. He knew that how much ever he spoke, they were not prepared to comprehend or make sense of what He intended to tell them. But He continued encouraging them and exhorting them to believe in what He had told them. This is what the Lord keeps reminding us each day – to trust Him, He will make sure that all is well, regardless of how adverse our current situations may seem. The reality may be that we may not be knowing the way ahead or where we are heading. The skies may have been forever yellow and disenchanting that we fail to recognize ourselves. Then we must ask the Lord, as one of his disciples Thomas asked: Lord we do not know where you are going (taking us) nor do we know the way. He will surely then give us a reply – one of assurance, conviction and comfort. Speak to us Jesus as we stray away from the path you take us through, remind us of your ongoing presence in our lives, especially in moments of despair, agony and fear. Be the Way, the Light and Life in our lives. Amen
