### Verse for Today

> ‘You know all things and that you do not even need to have anyone ask you questions. This makes us believe that you came from God’ (John 16:30)

---

### Reflection

The disciples expressed their faith in Jesus, that He was the Son of God the Father. This faith was however from their experience, from what they heard, saw, and learned all through their three years of living with the Lord, while He was on earth. But even so, they failed to hold on to this faith in Jesus, when crises erupted in life. (Jesus was abandoned by these disciples, when He was arrested, taken for trial, and sentenced to death.) If ‘these’ who walked, talked, listened, and lived with Jesus slipped from faith at one point in life (until the Spirit of the Lord came upon them in anointing), so would be for each one of us, who proclaim our love for the Lord, our commitment towards His call, and desire to be with Him always. Jesus knows it all – what we are, what we have been designed for, and where we stand today. So let us ask Him to pray for us that we do not slip from faith, that we become anointed by the power of His Spirit, and live to fulfill the will of God for us. It is this that we submit in prayer before Him, for ourselves and for all whom He has given to our care. Jesus be with us. Amen
