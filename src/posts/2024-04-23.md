### Verse for Today

> ‘ You have disposed all things by measure and number and weight’ (Wisdom 11:20)

---

### Reflection

Orderliness and Peace were foundational to this earth when it was established and set by the Spirit of God. Into this Orderliness was mankind sent, to live in harmony with nature and ensure its upkeep. God invited mankind into His Creativity to ordain peace and tranquility in all that they do and speak; to thwart all chaos and uncertainty that may emerge in the course of his living. Now in our lives today, if things that occur seem to create disruptions and disturbance, it’s time we turn to God who has disposed all things in their right measure and number, to ask Him for a turnaround. And this we are assured that it is not in mere human effort and desire that things change for the better but in God’s mercy and compassion. Jesus calls us to a life of peace by surrendering all that is ours to Him, who sustains the world at large. Towards harmony, peace and joy in the Holy Spirit, You and I have been called and set apart for by the Lord. Where else can our hope emerge from apart from the great promise that Jesus has given us? Wishing you a Spirit filled day! Amen
