### Verse for Today

> ‘You may have the strength to comprehend with all the saints what is the breadth and length and height and depth of the love of Christ’ (Ephesians 3:18)

---

### Reflection

The love by which the Universe, you and me, and all the created were architected, arrayed, and set in place, the love that has helped us thrive and grow to this day, the love that strengthened us to walk through troubled seas, the fire, and the mountains that were enormous and way beyond our capabilities, the love that persuaded us to give our best even when we were at our lowest ebbs, the love that taught us to flip our scripts and thoughts from those cultural patterns, daily realities, and pulse of this world…..the love of Jesus Christ. It is towards this love that the Spirit of the Lord attracts you today, to rewire yourself, reorient your likes, priorities, and preferences, to be ‘yourself’, the person you were created to be. The fact is that you are most precious, chosen, and have been adopted into the Heavenly Kingdom, in accordance with the desire of the Father, that in Jesus Christ, by being a new Creation through Him (moulded in love), you would inherit all the Heavenly riches in the Higher places. This is the breadth, length, height, and depth of God’s love for you……that He awaited to see you through, by comprehending how much He loved you. Let us entreat the Blessed Mary, our mother, to help us grow up this ladder of love of Jesus. Amen
