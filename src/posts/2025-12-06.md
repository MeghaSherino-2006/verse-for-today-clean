### Verse for Today

> ‘The Lord longs to be gracious to you; therefore, He will rise up to show you compassion’ (Isaiah 30:18)

---

### Reflection

The ‘forgiveness of sins’ and ‘redemption’ are the two gifts the Lord wills to shower upon you without measure. Not that we have earned His goodwill, but because He cares and loves us. The Bible tells us, ‘This is God’s love for us,’ not that we loved God, but that He loved us and thus sent His only Son as an atoning sacrifice for our sins. And Jesus commissioned His disciples by telling them to give freely what they freely received from Him. If God has forgiven your sins and shown you compassion, then reciprocate this goodwill to those around you – your dear and near – forgive and be merciful to them. Then your light will break forth like the dawn, and your healing will quickly appear; your righteousness shall go before you, and the glory of the Lord will be your rear guard, this is His promise for you today. A good measure, pressed down, shaken together, and running over, will be poured into your lap (Luke 6:38). Believe and retrieve this blessing in all spheres of your life, from the mighty arms of the Lord. Amen
