### Verse for Today

> ‘Great is the Lord, who delights in the welfare of His servant!’(Psalm 35:27)

---

### Reflection

The eyes of the Lord watch over all Creation – to see if there are those among men who honor Him, obey His Commandments, and hope in His compassion and forgiveness. These are graced with the Light of God’s favor. In all that they do, they prosper; they are blessed in abundance that too in the sight of all. It is He who protects them from the intrigues of men and lying tongues. We are children of a God who delights in our uplift, our well-being and our welfare. Not that there will not be rough patches in life as we keep moving; there might come situations, people and authorities who disown or fail to approve of our motives and ways. But the one who has formed us so special and distinct knows the length, breadth and dimensions of our life. Let us entrust ourselves to the Lord, seek His intervention in our lives, open our hearts to delight in His presence, to praise and thank Him for all goodness in life, to emit His mercy and love to all that is cold and fallen. I thank you Lord for being my refuge and extending your arms of protection for me. Yes there is no one like the God of Jeshurun, who secures his beloved to ride over the heights! Amen
