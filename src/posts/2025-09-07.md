### Verse for Today

> 'whoever says I abide in Him, ought to walk just as He walked' (1John 2:6)

---

### Reflection

He walked in the path of Light, His steps were firm even when the ground beneath appeared tardy and narrow. We who boldly claim to be His followers are called to tread the same path. But the fact remains, that we are so feeble, unable to move forward when the journey becomes long, tiring, and exhausting. Our hearts may fail us when fret by obstacles on the way. How enthusiastic we were when we began, trusting in the gifts that He bestowed to us? But when taunted by trials all the way, we were distraught, deprived of strength. Know this, that He was aware of us, of the desire in us, so He never let go of us. Somewhere down the lane, we lost our grip, but we were still under His radar. Jesus knows and cares for you - will never let you down. Trust and ask Him for the empowerment you would need to make this day one memorable and enriched. Let us ask the Blessed Mother to intercede for us in this regard. Amen
